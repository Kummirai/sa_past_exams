About MiltonTutor+

Empowering learners through accessible education

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate educators in Johannesburg, SA Past Exams began as a small initiative to help underprivileged students access quality study materials.

What started as a shared Google Drive among teachers has grown into South Africa's most comprehensive online repository of past exam papers, serving over 50,000 learners nationwide.

Today, we partner with schools across all nine provinces to collect, verify, and distribute curriculum-aligned resources to help students excel in their exams.

Learn About Our Impact

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality educational resources for all South African learners, regardless of their socioeconomic background or geographic location.

Our Vision

A South Africa where every learner has equal opportunity to excel academically through access to comprehensive, curriculum-aligned study materials.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Accessibility

We believe education should be available to all. That's why all our resources are completely free, with no hidden costs or premium paywalls.

Quality

Every exam paper is carefully reviewed by our team of educators to ensure accuracy and alignment with the CAPS curriculum.

Community

We're built by educators for learners. Our platform thrives on contributions from teachers across South Africa.

Our Journey

Milestones in our educational mission

2015 - Humble Beginnings

Founded by three teachers sharing resources at a Johannesburg high school. Started with just 200 exam papers.

2017 - First Website Launch

Launched our first online platform with 1,500 exam papers. Reached 5,000 users in the first year.

2019 - Provincial Expansion

Partnered with schools in all nine provinces. Content grew to over 10,000 verified exam papers.

2021 - Mobile App Launch

Released our Android and iOS apps, making resources accessible to learners without computers.

2023 - Current Reach

Now serving over 50,000 registered users with more than 15,000 exam papers and study resources.
