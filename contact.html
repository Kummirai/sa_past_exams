About SA Past Exams

Empowering South African learners through quality educational resources

Our Mission

SA Past Exams was founded in 2015 with a simple goal: to make quality educational resources accessible to all South African learners, regardless of their background or location.

We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to excel in their exams, and that practice with real past papers is one of the most effective ways to prepare.

Our platform collects, organizes, and provides exam papers from schools across all nine provinces, ensuring comprehensive coverage of the CAPS curriculum.

What We Offer

  • Past exam papers for Grades 7-12
  • CAPS-aligned question banks
  • Official marking memorandums
  • Study guides and revision resources
  • Mobile-friendly access
